DOLOR SIT
Due to the effects of the pandemic, DiversityInc had to take their annual all-day event and take it virtual. The 2020 DiversityInc Top 50 Companies for Diversity is an important event that highlights companies taking initiative to increase representation across their respective companies. The annual event was approaching within a ten day timeframe and needed to be reconceptualized from a grand ballroom in-person style event to a socially-distant virtual one.
SOLUTION
Geniecast mobilized to produce a multilingual event with moderators, technical audits, a full seminar schedule, and networking sessions. In ten days, Geniecast was able to rescope the event into a series of keynotes, break out rooms, and working sessions. Translators as well as ASL interpreters were booked and prepped to provide real-time translations.Dozens of technical audits were carried out for all the speakers to ensure that video and audio were of the highest quality, with no downtime or disruption.
  • Speaker Prep and Coaching
  • Technical Audits
  • Attendee Management
  • Pre-Event Rehearsal
The conference capped off with a virtual gala and an announcement of the DiversityInc top 50 for 2020 was successfully executed.
